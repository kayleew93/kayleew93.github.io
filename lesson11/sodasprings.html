Residents asked to reduce water use

Due to the unprecendented drought we are experiencing this season, effective immediately the following city ordinances will be put into place. First, all residents are asked to limit the use of water in homes. This may include shortening the length of your typical shower, reduce the amount of water used while washing dishes, and only wash clothing when the load is full. Second, long watering and maintenance should be limited to 20 minutes per day. In addition, please water your lawns outside of the hours of 10:00am to 4:00pm. Violators will be fined.
