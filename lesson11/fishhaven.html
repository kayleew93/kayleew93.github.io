Residents asked to limit fireworks

This patriotic season is a time where many of us enjoy the time-old tradition of lighting fireworks and enjoying the myriad of explosions and effects they produce. However, these holiday novelties can also be dangerous and the cause of some of the worst wildfires we see each year. Some of these fires are responsible for consuming homes and causing millions of dollars worth of property damage. Therefore, it is incument on each of us to practice caution and prudence when engaging in this otherwise delightful tradition.
