Target Audience

The target audience will be mainly adults living in eastern Idaho. They are comfortable using technology to access news and weather information. Typical site visitor age ranges between 18 - 50 years old. Most site visitors are educated, having gone to some college or full degrees.

According to Pew Research Center:

  • 34% of United States adults would rather get their news online
  • 37% of US adults prefer to get their local news online
  • 82% of US adults who view their local news online value an easy-to-use website
  • 77% of US adults indicate internet is important for accessing local news

  • 46 years old
  • Married
  • Father of four children
  • Has a bachelor's in computer programming
  • Annual salary: $100,000
Goals and tasks: He has a moderate commute, and relies on weather reports to plan travel. He also enjoys sports and is a big fan of his alma mater's team.

Other Potential Scenarios

  • Do I need to bring a jacket or coat to work?
