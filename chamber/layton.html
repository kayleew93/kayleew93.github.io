Layton City

History

Layton was settled around 1850 by pioneers from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It gets its name from Christopher Layton. Christopher had settled in the area and produced alfalfa crops.

Current Demographics

  • Most Common Language: 87.6% English
  • Poverty Rate: 7.31%
  • Marriage Rate: 58.7%
  • Number of Veterans: 4,643
  • Population Annual Growth Rate: 1.06%
